Cooley

Cooley is a discord bot that helps you play Dungeons and Dragons with friends! He stores character sheets and stats so you can listen to the Dungeon Master in the voice chat! Written in C#, Cooley keeps your character sheets in JSON for whenever you need them on your discord server!

ZCoder

My therapist told me I needed to start journaling to practice being vulnerable, so I wrote a program to loosely encrypt what I write. ZCoder is a Java project that converts the text in files to and from binary text, and my therapist says it was a great first step!

maeserflags.com

This is a website that features flags designed by students at Karl G. Maeser Preperatory Academy in Lindon, UT in a Vexilology class. The website was designed by a team of students in a Web Design class, lead by yours truly, as our first group website.
